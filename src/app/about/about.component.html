ClaimsKG Statistical Observatory

The ClaimsKG Statistical Observatory is a complementary tool to the ClaimsKG search engine. It generates graphs and statistics from the data in the ClaimsKG knowledge graph.

This statistical observatory allows for the visual exploration of various metrics and trends related to claims and their verification across the different fact-checking websites included in ClaimsKG.

Key Features:

  • Visualization of the total number of claims and fact-checking reviews over time
  • Distribution of truth evaluations (true, false, misleading, etc.)
  • Analysis of the frequency of appearance of different entities (people, organizations, locations, etc.) associated with the claims
  • Evolution of the number of claims by thematic category (politics, health, economy, etc.)
  • Comparison of the performance of different fact-checking websites in terms of coverage volume and results

About the Project

This statistical analysis tool aims to facilitate the understanding of large-scale fact-checking dynamics and trends, relying on the structured and enriched data from the ClaimsKG project.

Just like the ClaimsKG search engine, the statistical observatory is the result of a collaborative effort between French and German research teams. It is made available to the public for research purposes, while respecting the copyrights of the source fact-checking websites.
